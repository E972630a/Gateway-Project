Looking for an IT position?

Do not worry Finland has several job opportunities in the IT department. There are several IT consultant firms (e.g. Bitfactor,Columbia Road, and more).

Bitfactor

Location(s): Helsinki, Finland; Oulu, Finland

Why Bitfactor?: A design driven consulting company mastering design, software engineering and analytics. Big clients and interesting cases.


Futurice

Location(s): Helsinki, Finland

Why Futurice?: They are a company with strong and ambitious vision. Everyone there is equal as it should be everywhere. They have a strong support system. They allow their employees power to make decisions, are open and transparent.


Eficode

Location(s):Helsinki, Finland; Tampere, Finland

Why Eficode?: They have 300 experts in DevOps, UX, and software development! Experience a friendly and supportive work environment with great benefits and the space to grow your career.


Reaktor

Location(s):Helsinki, Finland; Turku, Finland

Why Reaktor?: Is an all star consulting company and developers' all time favorite. They are an organization built on empathy, respect, and trust. Collaborate and support each other.
